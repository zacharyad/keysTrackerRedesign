Welcome to 13 Keys Tracker!

This is a free interactive tool for predicting the winner of the 2024 U.S. presidential election, based on Professor Allan Lichtman's famous 13 Keys to the White House.

  • Using his key system, he correctly predicted the outcome of every presidential election since 1860!
  • The keys are a checklist of True/False statements that pertain to the circumstances surrounding a presidential election.
  • You can see where the keys currently stand based on Lichtman's most recent public comments in the key descriptions.
  • Political colors are not assigned, but red and blue are being used regardless. Any historical races may not correlate to party leanings based on color.
  • You can also make your own prediction by clicking the boxes to cycle through assignments of True/False labels to the keys.
  • Modify the end of the URL with a /year to get historical keys (e.g. "www.13KeysInteractive.com/1988") to get historical keys to those races.
